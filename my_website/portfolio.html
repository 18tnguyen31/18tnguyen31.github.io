Tommy's Portfolio




The purpose of my game is to score as many points as possible, and points are earned by
accurately pressing on the corresponding arrows to when the guitars hit the "scoring row."
To make my game, I started off with the technique to score points. It began with how the guitars
would move upward, and it progressed to the points which would be added. When start is received, the
guitars will return to their original position then glide upward in the time determined by the difficulty.
To score, the player would have to have the correct arrow key pressed when the moving guitars are touching
the stationary ones. If the key is correctly pressed, the score is changed by +10. When it's pressed and not
touching, it would subtract 5 from the score. The most difficult thing about my game was moving the guitars.
I solved this issue by "taking inspiration" from another game. I had an opportunity to make a dank ending
screen so I used presidents :/
